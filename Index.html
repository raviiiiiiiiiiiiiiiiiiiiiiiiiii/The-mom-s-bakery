FAQ

How long in advance should I order?
We recommend 3 days for standard cakes and 5–7 days for complex showpieces.
Do you deliver?
Yes — local delivery is available. Message us on WhatsApp for delivery fees and slots.
Are your cakes nut-free?
We use almond and nut-based ingredients for some items — contact us for nut-free options and we’ll advise alternatives.

How to order

  1. Pick a cake or describe your custom idea.
  2. Send size, date, and any reference images via WhatsApp.
  3. We confirm design, price, and delivery/pickup details.
  4. Pay via UPI or cash on pickup/delivery as arranged.
